---
title: Transfer packet checklist
slug: transfer-packet
summary: Documents needed before scheduling a closing meeting.
order: 2
---

1. Executed bill of sale and inventory list.
2. Conditional use or zoning letter from local jurisdiction.
3. Responsible Vendor Program training attestations.
4. Proof of tax clearance from the Department of Revenue.

Upload all documents to the document helper and note extraction tasks in the audit log for accountability.
