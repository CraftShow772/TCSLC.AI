---
title: What documents are required to transfer a license?
slug: title-transfer
summary: Checklist for completing a title transfer without delays.
order: 2
---

Prepare the following before requesting a transfer review:

- Executed bill of sale with notarized signatures.
- Zoning compliance letter dated within 30 days.
- Responsible Vendor Program training certificates for managers.
- LiveScan fingerprint receipts for all controlling parties.

The assistant references these requirements when you mention buying or selling a business.
