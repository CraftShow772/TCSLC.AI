## Pre-application planning

1. Reserve a location and obtain a zoning compatibility letter.
2. Schedule Responsible Vendor Program training for managers.
3. Prepare a source-of-funds narrative with bank statements for the past six months.

## Submission checklist

- Complete the Department of Revenue DR-1 registration.
- Submit LiveScan fingerprints for all officers and managing members.
- Upload floor plans with designated service areas highlighted.

The assistant surfaces this guidance when you ask about starting a new business, ensuring consistent answers across channels.
